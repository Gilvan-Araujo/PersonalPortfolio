About the app

The United Stated of America currency model has common names for each of its cents coins: penny for 1 cent, nickel for 5, dime for 10, quarter for 25, half dollar for 50 and the whole dollar coin.

This application's purpose is to allow the user to input a value in dollars in the text field, convert it to the equivalent integer amount in cents. together with the total of coins taking the four firs first four coins in mind (pennies, nickels, dimes and quarters). After this, convert into coins with the sub-type of dollars: penny, nickel, dime and quarter and output few coins as possible.
