About the app

Anyone that has ever tried hosting a barbecue, knows that you seem to never find that sweet spot; either you always fall short on the meat and drinks, or you purchase to much and it goes to waste.

Thinking about that, this app was developed with a formula in mind, which takes into consideration the number of adults, children and the duration of the barbecue in hours, and returns an estimate kilograms of meat, liters of beer and liters of other drinks, such as soda or water, for the kids.
